Frequently Asked Questions

How do I restore my premium purchases?

If you reinstalled the app or set up a new device, you can restore your purchases by opening the app, navigating to the Settings screen, and tapping the "Restore Purchases" button. Make sure you are signed in with the same App Store/Google Play account used for the original purchase.

I bought an item but it hasn't appeared. What should I do?

First, verify that your payment was successful in your Apple or Google account purchase history. Try restarting the app with a stable internet connection. If the item still does not show, tap the "Restore Purchases" button in the Settings menu or contact us below with your transaction ID.

Why are rewarded video ads failing to load?

Ads may fail to load due to poor internet connectivity, active adblockers, or temporary shortages of ads from the Google AdMob network. Ensure you have a stable network connection and try again.
